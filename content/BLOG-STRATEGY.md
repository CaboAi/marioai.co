# Blog Strategy: marioai.co

## Business Profile
- **Business**: AI integrations consultant (websites, workflow automation, AI strategy)
- **Location**: Cabo San Lucas, Mexico + serving US clients (primarily California / Los Angeles)
- **Target market**: Small businesses in Los Cabos AND the US (especially CA/LA) — tourism, hospitality, real estate, healthcare, service businesses
- **Languages**: English and Spanish (bilingual market)
- **Differentiators**: Real client results with metrics, local market knowledge, build-in-public transparency

## Goals
- Organic SEO traffic for AI automation keywords
- Lead generation (drive discovery call bookings)
- Thought leadership / authority in AI for small business
- AI citation presence (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)

## Competitive Landscape
- **No local competitors** in Los Cabos for AI consulting
- **National competitors to outrank**:
  - theautomators.ai — broad AI automation content
  - lonelypine.ai — hospitality-specific AI workflows
  - labattsimon.com — AI consulting for small businesses
  - aismartventures.com — AI tools roundups
- **Key gap**: Nobody writes about AI automation for Mexican tourism/hospitality businesses from a practitioner's perspective with real case studies

## Audience Segments

### Segment 1: Small Business Owner — US (Primary)
- Service business, restaurant, or agency owner in California / Los Angeles
- Pain: drowning in admin, manual processes, wants to scale without hiring
- Searches: "AI automation for small business", "workflow automation Los Angeles", "AI consultant near me"
- Buying stage: Awareness -> Consideration

### Segment 1b: Local Business Owner — Los Cabos
- Restaurant, hotel, spa, or rental property owner in Los Cabos
- Pain: manual booking/invoicing, no-shows, lead leakage
- Searches: "how to automate restaurant bookings", "AI for small business Mexico"
- Buying stage: Awareness -> Consideration

### Segment 2: Digital-Forward Entrepreneur
- Runs an online business or service company, tech-curious
- Pain: wants to scale without hiring, looking for AI tools
- Searches: "best AI automation tools 2026", "workflow automation for service business"
- Buying stage: Consideration -> Decision

### Segment 3: US/Canada Expat Business Owner in Mexico
- Runs a business in Cabo, comfortable with English content
- Pain: finding reliable tech partners in Mexico, bridging US tools with local operations
- Searches: "tech consultant Los Cabos", "AI solutions Baja California"
- Buying stage: Awareness -> Decision
